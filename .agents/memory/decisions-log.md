# Architectural & Design Decisions Log

- **Theme Transition**: Upgraded the dark background hex from a pure generic black `#09090B` to a warm, expensive deep espresso `#1C1916`. This perfectly compliments the tropical modernism and raw travertine theme of the site.
- **Act III Mechanics**: Shifted from crossfading background videos to an infinite-scroll vertical image carousel paired with fading poetry text. This grounds abstract brand claims in recognizable, high-end "room" realities (Living, Bedroom, Dining).
- **Act IV Mechanics**: Adopted the "Negative / Large-Type Hybrid" footer pattern from the UI/UX skills reference. Integrated `THRESHOLD` as a 22vw, 8% opacity architectural watermark bleeding off the bottom of the section.
- **CTA Specificity**: Enforced solid ivory backgrounds with dark text on primary inquiry capsules using `!important` declarations to break free from the global glassmorphism styling hierarchy.
- **Inquiry Page Layout Priority**: Structured the page to show the "Engagements" (Pricing columns) first, immediately after the hero, before going into the "What's in a build" services breakdown.
- **Motion Copying Ban**: Enacted a strict rule against verbatim copying of reference motion patterns (like Good Fella's dot wave). Instead, references are reverse-engineered, added to the Motion Design Library for future inspiration, and replaced with bespoke, original Awwwards-style interactions in production (e.g., Hover Blur & Focus).
- **Typography Sizing Constraint**: Reduced header and list typography sizing on the Inquiry page by over 30% from initial drafts to match the premium, restrained editorial aesthetic of high-end spatial studios.
- **Typography System Refinement**: Reverted the edgy "Syne" experiment in favor of an architectural "Jost" + editorial "Cormorant Garamond" + legible "Manrope" stack. The font should frame the space, not distract from it.
- **Color System Reset (BEVAMPED Concept)**: Replaced the generic tech-startup Navy (`#1A1F36`) with a luxury Gallery Obsidian (`#0A0A0B`) as the primary dark mode background. Conceptually paired with "Cool Plaster" (`#F5F6F8`) for structure and Gold (`#D4AF37`) for the exact moment of premium transformation.

- **Interaction Meaning**: Shifted hover-based interactions to click-based interactions (e.g., FAQ text scramble) to enforce intentionality ("decoding") over chaotic reactive states, bringing the interaction model closer to premium reference sites.
- **Micro-Interaction Styling**: Scrambled cipher characters use the --vamped-gold color against a dark background to emphasize the brand's premium/luxury feel.
- **Accordion Architecture**: Avoided native HTML <details> element constraints by building a custom accordion using <div> wrappers and display: grid with  fr to 1fr transitions for buttery smooth height expansion without JavaScript calculation.

- **Typography Scaling**: Scaled back the brutalist typography sizing (clamp(3rem, 6vw, 6rem) -> clamp(2rem, 3.5vw, 3rem)) for sub-headings to restore a luxurious, constrained editorial feel and enforce clear section hierarchy.
- **Logo Navigation**: Engineered the logo transformation in the navigation bar to accurately emulate a 3D flip (using transform-style: preserve-3d), revealing a high-contrast white wordmark on the back face when the menu is active.
- **Root URL Resolution & Custom Domain**: Configured Name.com DNS and Vercel hosting to serve the site directly on the root `bevamped.studio` domain. Renamed `inquiry.html` to `index.html` to eliminate trailing `.html` requirements on the primary entry page.
- **Separate Standalone Legal Pages**: Migrated privacy policy and legal notice modals into dedicated, crawlable `/privacy` and `/legal` subpages rather than inline popups. Used Vite multi-page routing configurations to package these targets cleanly.
- **Vertical Shutter Page Transitions**: Custom-built a GSAP vertical shutter-wipe transition (`src/legal-page.ts` and `src/inquiry.ts`) that intercepts navigation clicks on `.transition-link`, closes the curtain, redirects the browser, and then slides open the shutter on the destination page to maintain the cinematic visual continuity.
- **iOS Safari Touch Target Fix**: Added `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.001)` background and `width: 100%` on mobile flex containers to resolve the flex-gap transparent touch target glitch.
- **Dark Navbar Styling**: Configured a `.dark-nav` selector to force navbar items (logo and menu buttons) to render in `var(--vamped-ivory)` on dark-themed legal pages, ensuring perfect visibility and scrolling blur/color states.
- **Editorial Heading & List Numbering**: Redesigned legal list numbers to sit side-by-side with headings in a horizontal flexbox layout `.legal-section-header` featuring a bottom border, replacing standard list numbering with a large, italicized serif number `01/` using `--vamped-serif` (Cormorant Garamond) for a high-end print-magazine aesthetic.
- **Bespoke Back Button**: Upgraded the standard text link for "Back to Showcase" to a pill-shaped, semi-transparent outline button with custom elongated SVG arrow, featuring horizontal slide micro-interactions on hover.
