Privacy Policy

Back to Showcase

This policy outlines how we handle data on our digital showcase, ensuring your privacy is respected. Restraint is our signal—this extends directly to data collection.

01/

Controller

BEVAMPED is responsible for the processing of personal data on this website. If you have any inquiries regarding data protection or wish to request data deletion, contact us directly at hello@bevamped.studio.

02/

No Invasive Tracking

Unlike standard SaaS sites, we do not use invasive cookie-based analytics, Facebook tracking pixels, or marketing scripts to follow your movement across the web. We run a privacy-first, cookieless setup. No cookies are stored on your device, and no personal data is persistently logged when browsing this portfolio.

03/

Booking System (Cal.com)

We use Cal.com to schedule diagnostic sessions and project calls. When you schedule a call, Cal.com collects your name, email, timezone, and project brief details solely for the purpose of executing the meeting and pre-contractual performance.

04/

Direct Contact Form & Channels

When you contact us via our site qualification brief, email, WhatsApp, or Instagram, we collect the details you voluntarily provide (name, contact handles, budget range, and project brief). This data is processed strictly to review your project scope and coordinate slots in our calendar.

05/

Hosting (Vercel)

Our website is hosted on Vercel. Vercel automatically generates server logs that include IP addresses, browser types, and access timestamps to ensure the security, integrity, and performance of our infrastructure.

06/

Your Data Rights

Under global data protection laws (including GDPR), you retain the right to:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify inaccurate information.
  • Erase your project briefs and contact details from our records.
  • Restrict processing or object to our communications.
To exercise these rights, please email us directly at hello@bevamped.studio. We will process all deletion requests within 24 hours.
